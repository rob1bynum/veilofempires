# Protocol Rank Allocation

## Rank 1
- Arcane Rank 1
- 3 Protocol Points
- No Protocol may exceed Rank 1

## Rank 2
- Arcane Rank 2
- 4 Protocol Points
- No Protocol may exceed Rank 2

Each selected Protocol Rank costs one Protocol Point. Example legal Rank 2 loadout:

- Ghost Step II: 2 points
- Iron Heart I: 1 point
- Arcane Sight I: 1 point
- Total: 4 points

Primary and additional Protocol slots all count against the same budget.
