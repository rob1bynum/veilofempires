Attribute Guide

Strength

Can I overpower it? Raw physical power for lifting, breaking, grappling, climbing, and forceful close combat.

Dexterity

Can I manipulate it precisely? Fine control for ranged attacks, medicine, engineering, technology, and delicate manual work.

Endurance

Can I endure it? Toughness and stamina against injury, fatigue, poison, disease, and hostile environments.

Awareness

Can I notice or understand it? Observation, intuition, investigation, tracking, and recognition of arcane phenomena.

Agility

Can I avoid or outmaneuver it? Reflexes, balance, stealth, acrobatics, initiative, movement, and evasion.

Presence

Can I influence it? Confidence, determination, leadership, persuasion, deception, intimidation, and animal handling.

Compatibility

Character data is compatible with the v2.25.0 baseline.

The 3.0 sheet retains the established Roll20 attribute names used by existing Agents. New NPC, Director, and release fields are additive.

Expansion content can register new records through the stable extension registry without replacing the core rules engine.
