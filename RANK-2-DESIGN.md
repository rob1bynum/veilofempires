# Rank 2 Progression

## Cost
Advancing from Rank 1 to Rank 2 costs 1,000 current XP.

## Universal rewards
- +5 to one Attribute
- +5 to two different Skills
- +5 Maximum Health
- Rank 2 Protocol effects become active

## Specialty choice
Choose one:
1. Advance the current Specialty to Rank 2.
2. Gain a different Specialty at Rank 1.

### Rank 2 Specialty benefits
Each Rank 2 Specialty adds +5 to two signature Skills, +1 Contact, an upgraded feature, and for Vanguard/Warden an additional +5 Maximum Health.
