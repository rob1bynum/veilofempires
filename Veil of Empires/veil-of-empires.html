Combat Reference

Actions

3 actions at the start of each round.

Move: 1 action.

Attack: 1 action.

Multiple Attacks

First attack: no penalty.

Second attack: -10%.

Third attack: -20%.

Armor

Light: attacker -5%.

Medium: attacker -10%.

Heavy: attacker -15%.

Evasion

Spend your Reaction and roll Athletics before the attack.

Success: attacker suffers -10%.

Health

All Agents begin with 10 Health.

Temporary Health is lost before normal Health.

Ursa and Vanguard each add +5 Maximum Health.

Flip the digits of the last d100 result. Do not reroll.
